01 The concept

Politiplay is a fantasy politics league — draft 11 real-world public figures onto your squad, earn points when they make news, win your weekly Sweep. Modeled on the mechanics of fantasy football, designed with the gravitas of global affairs.

A game on the surface. A crowd-sourced political intelligence platform underneath. The game drives the data. The data drives the business.

Every draft pick, waiver move, and Captain selection is a data signal. At scale, Politiplay generates a political intelligence dataset that is genuinely valuable to pollsters, newsrooms, campaign staff, and researchers — a B2B intelligence layer built on top of a consumer game.

02 How it works
STEP 01
Draft your squad
Pick 11 public figures across 5 position types. One Captain scores 2×. Two Wildcards — chaos agents — score 1.5×. No bench: all 11 score every week.
Captain 2× · Wildcard 1.5×
STEP 02
They make news
Election wins, legislation passed, treaties signed, approval swings, scandals, indictments. Seven independent feeds score events automatically. No predictions required — just good picks.
Indictment −25 · Scandal −18
STEP 03
Win the Sweep
Lineups lock Monday. Events score live Mon–Fri. Winner announced Sunday. Every player competes in their regional league and the global board simultaneously.
Double Sweep = rarest win
